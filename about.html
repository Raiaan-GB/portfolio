About This Website

Helping students and staff recover and return lost items on campus

What is Lost & Found?

Lost & Found is a student-built web application for reporting and recovering lost items within the University of Buckingham campus. It allows students and staff to submit lost or found item reports, browse listings, and view directions to campus locations.

How to Use This Website:

  • Step 1: If you lost something, go to Report Lost and fill out the form.
  • Step 2: If you found something, submit it through Report Found.
  • Step 3: Browse all listings on the Browse page.
  • Step 4: Use Campus Directions to locate buildings where items may be returned.
  • Step 5: Submit feedback or suggestions on the Feedback page.

Technologies Used:

  • HTML5 + CSS3
  • Bootstrap 5 for responsive design
  • JavaScript for DOM manipulation and interactivity
  • localStorage for saving lost, found and feedback forms
